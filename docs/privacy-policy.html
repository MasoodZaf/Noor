Privacy Policy

Falah — Islamic companion app · Effective 7 May 2026
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Falah runs entirely on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no servers operated by us hold any of your data.

1. What this means in practice

  • You don't sign in. There is no Falah account.
  • We do not maintain user databases. We have no way to identify you.
  • We do not run analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell, rent, share, or monetise any information.
  • Anything you save (bookmarks, prayer settings, Hifz progress, recordings) is stored only on your device and is removed when you delete the app.

2. Information stored on your device

To function offline, Falah keeps the following locally on your device only. None of it leaves your device or reaches us.

  • Quran, Hadith, and Dua text (bundled SQLite database).
  • Cached prayer times, Hijri calendar, and Qibla bearing.
  • Bookmarks, Hifz spaced-repetition progress, language preference, theme, notification preferences, and reciter selection.
  • Your most recent recitation audio (used only for in-app playback; a new recording overwrites the previous one).

All of the above lives in the operating-system sandbox for the Falah app. Uninstalling Falah deletes it.

3. Permissions Falah may request

The app asks for the minimum permissions needed for each feature. Granting them is optional; denying them only disables the related feature.

4. Third-party services Falah connects to

Falah needs the public internet to fetch Islamic content and prayer times. These requests are made directly from your device to the providers below. Each provider has its own privacy policy; we do not control how they process the technical request data (such as your IP address) that any HTTP request necessarily exposes.

ServiceUsed forData sent
AlAdhan (aladhan.com) Prayer times, Hijri calendar, Qibla bearing. Approximate latitude / longitude (or chosen city), date, calculation method.
Quran.com / AlQuran Cloud / Fawaz Quran CDN Quran text, translations, recitation audio. Surah / ayah numbers requested.
Fawaz Hadith CDN Hadith collections. Collection / hadith identifiers.
Qurani.ai Optional AI search and recitation feedback. Your search query, or — only if you tap "Record" — the audio of that recitation. Disabled if no API key is configured.
mp3quran CDN Reciter audio playback. Surah identifier of the file requested.
Unsplash (images.unsplash.com) Decorative imagery for category cards. Standard image-fetch request only.

No identifiers, accounts, device IDs, or advertising IDs are attached to any of these requests by Falah. The providers may log routine technical metadata (IP, user-agent, timestamp) the way any web request does. If that concerns you, you can reduce reliance on the network by enabling Offline Mode in Settings.

5. Children's privacy

Falah is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any data from any user, we cannot — and do not — collect data from children. The app does not profile users, serve advertising, or include third-party tracking.

6. Your choices and controls

  • Offline Mode — turn off all outbound network requests once content has been cached.
  • Permissions — revoke any permission at any time in your device's system settings; the corresponding feature will simply be skipped.
  • Notifications — toggle prayer-time, daily-ayah, and Friday Kahf reminders in the app or system settings.
  • Reset — uninstalling Falah removes all locally stored data, since nothing is held off-device.

7. Your rights under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA

Privacy laws such as the GDPR and the CCPA grant rights to access, correct, delete, or port personal data held by a service. Because Falah holds no personal data about you on any server we operate, there is nothing for us to access, correct, delete, or port. Local data on your device is fully under your control via the Reset option above.

8. Security

Network requests use HTTPS where the upstream provider supports it. Local data is sandboxed by your operating system to the Falah app. No data is encrypted at rest by Falah beyond what the OS provides, because no sensitive data is stored.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make any change that affects how Falah handles information, we will update this page and revise the effective date at the top. Material changes will also be flagged in the app's release notes.
